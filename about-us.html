Overview

Forward Private Equity focuses primarily on small to medium sized UK based companies that have achieved profitability and are looking to develop their business further to take advantage of growth opportunities.

Our successful heritage has focused on the digital consumer and internet industries. We will continue to invest in those sectors whilst broadening our reach into other areas of TMT as well as business services and consumer services, all of which are impacted by the digital transformation.

What We Do

We invest in and support small and medium sized businesses.

  • We primarily look for controlling equity positions in businesses that offer significant growth potential — but our equity stake size can be flexible

  • Our team has extensive experience as entrepreneurs, business leaders, investors and consultants, and therefore we welcome innovative and complex transactions

  • Typically focused on MBIs/MBOs, divestitures, carve-outs, growth capital and turnarounds

  • Primarily focused on UK-domiciled businesses but we also consider Western European and US businesses
